75 Days of Productivity

June, 2022

This isn’t a known challenge, but more like a mix of two other challenges that you may, or may not have heard of. The first one is the 75 day challenge. Read about it here! Short summary, drink one gallon of water, workout 90 minutes, follow a diet, no alcohol, read 10 pages of a non-fiction book,and take a progress picture each day!

The second one is one I actually found on Tumblr. This one is called 100 days of productivity. There isn’t too much to tell about this one besides each day do one productive thing! On Tumblr, most people like to do daily updates, which I will try to do here. I say try because I am graduating soon and don’t know how it will be for the next 75 days, but I will try my hardest!

What am I doing for 75 days?

  • Drink lots of water (gallon)
  • Workout daily
  • Meditation twice a day
  • Skin care twice a day
  • Reading/Journaling
  • 1 productive thing
  • Make 1 collage of four pictures

Come back soon to see my updates and progress!

Planner Process

June 2022

My online planner is currently still in the works, but I am making great progress! Hopefully I will be able to have the entire thing finished by the end of the summer!

Now what is my online planner?

I wanted a place where I can organize my life. The things I am most excited for in my project are...

  1. Easily displaying daily, monthly and yearly tasks, goals, and chores
  2. Having an organized project page where I can see all my projects displayed, their tasks, due dates, and end goal.
  3. Having an organized meal prep, meal plan, and shopping list.
Since my partner, Jon, works as a DJ, I’d also like to add a DJ scheduler for him, so he can easily plan out his work days and I can also see what he is doing and make plans around them!

The hardest thing about this project so far is working by myself to make sure everything is cohesive and well planned out to make things easier for me while coding, but also while I am using the website.

My favorite part is designing and seeing my project come to life. It’s really neat to see my idea come to life and think about how great and organized I will be once I finish.

Basically, I want a planner, a planner that encapsulates all. Eventually, I’d like to be able to put my planner online for my friends and family, but for now, it's exclusively on my local computer. If you’d like to view my code, or see my progress, feel free to contact me and I would love to share my GitHub!

Travelify Blog

Fall 2021 - Spring 2022

Travelify started in the fall semester of 2021, and it was finished in April, 2022. During that time, I developed my PHP and my SQL skills, and, dare I say, became quiet good at them both! The features I completed include

  • User Profile
  • Google Login/Logout implementation
  • Group Features
  • View and Edit Trip
  • Itinerary
  • Packing List
  • Calendar
While it was stressful at some times, it was a great experience and it taught me almost everything I know about PHP! It was so fun to make and further proved my love for creating and designing websites.
